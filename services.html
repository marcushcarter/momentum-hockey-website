SERVICES

Advising

The hockey world is a difficult one to navigate if you don’t know where to start– which is where we come in. With experience working at the elite level of hockey, our staff knows what it takes to reach the next level, and are committed to helping players chase their goals. Through providing understandable advice and touching base with potential suitors at the next level, Momentum Hockey aims to streamline the process of advancing for players and families, while removing the added pressure so they can focus solely on the season at hand.

Scouting

With years of experience in the scouting industry, our staff aims to uncover the diamonds in the rough, finding things to like about the most underrated players in the game. Through a well-refined process of identifying and analyzing the impact of talent, Momentum Hockey aims to find and assist players who deserve more visibility than they get. With detailed, analytical scouting reports, we hope to generate traction and get players seen by the decision-makers at the next level.

Developing

Utilizing a unique and understandable approach to player development, Momentum Hockey uses an analytical model that can help determine strengths and weaknesses in a player's game and provide actionable development plans to improve our clients’ on-ice impact. Not only do we strive to develop elite hockey players, but we also look to help our athletes grow into even better people, helping build up players that teams are proud to call their own.
